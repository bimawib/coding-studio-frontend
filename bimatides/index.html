BimaTides - Tide Monitoring Instrument

Continuously monitor the tidal conditions with bimatides. This tide gauge based on Arduino and IoT can measure the height of sea levels with high accuracy and can be monitored directly through the internet.

MPU-6050 Sensor

WiFi Module ESP8266
